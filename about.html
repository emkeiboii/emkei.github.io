about me

born and raised in italy, bologna. mark attended graphic school, but looking for a new challenge he started learning web development. although difficult he believes in conquering everything his mind has set itself to.

through graphic school he learned adobe illustrator, adobe photoshop and adobe indesign.

through online courses he learnt html and css, while still figuring out javascript.

when not bashing his keyboard, he may be at the gym, in the kitchen or at the park.

whatever you see, is the reflection of his time and effort, learning, succeeding and failing.
